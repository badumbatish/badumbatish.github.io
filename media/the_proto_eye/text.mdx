## Introduction

> A young boy walks away from home after a quarrel with his father at night and later gets kidnapped. He finds himself in a strange laboratory after waking up, facing a challenge…

Ahh, what a fine manhua. You know, growing up, my parents always encouraged and maybe even force me to learn Chinese
in order to keep our Chinese heritage alive. I was always more interested in Vietnamese and English more so unfortunately
I never could read or write in Chinese. The regret of not learning Chinese properly actually didn't come from the fact that
I couldn't use Chinese to converse with my extended family or with my Chinese friends, but from the fact that this specific
manhua, with its 268 chapters in Vietnamese, and around 120 chapters in US, is permanently discontinued, leaving me
unable to continue the story as its main language is Chinese.

I still remembered reading this manga in 2017/2018 back in Viet Nam on my Asus laptop. Oh those were the days ain't it hahahha.
It is truly a fine philosophical, sci-fi piece of manhua.
I recommend you have a read if you know Chinese; lest you're left vying for more when the chapters end abruptedly.
